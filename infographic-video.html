THE URBAN COYOTE

For this project, my goal was to create an infographic video that shared information while remaining engaging visually. I ultimately decided to make the subject of my project the urban coyote, a group of animals present in almost every city, and oftentimes hidden in plain sight. I wanted the visual style to be comical and bold, incorporating type within the environments. My assets for this project were made in procreate and exported to Adobe After Effects where I composited and animated the scenes.
