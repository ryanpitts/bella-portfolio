THE URBAN COYOTE

For this project, my goal was to create an infographic video that shared information while remaining engaging visually. I ultimately decided to make the subject of my project the urban coyote, a group of animals present in almost every city, and oftentimes hidden in plain sight. I began my process for this project with a moodboard, followed by a series of sketches that would help me define my visual style. I then created various thumbnails to nail down the narrative of my infographic, which allowed me to create refined style frames that were even further refined and given motion. All of this process resulted in the above infographic.
